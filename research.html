Research Interests

My current interests include:

  • Finite p-groups and their lower/upper central series.
  • Abelian words, Hall bases, and associated graded Lie rings.
  • Reducts of groups and conditions for verbal abelianness.
  • Representation theory of small groups (e.g., S3, D4) and beyond.
